# Module: The Goblin Cave
# System: D&D 5th Edition Solo Adventure
# Setting: The Sword Coast, Forgotten Realms

## OVERVIEW
- A merchant caravan has been raided on the Triboar Trail between Neverwinter and Phandalin.
- The player is a lone adventurer hired to investigate.
- They discover goblin tracks leading to a cave hideout in the forest.
- The cave is home to a small goblin tribe led by a bugbear named Klarg.
- Winning conditions: Defeat or negotiate with Klarg, rescue the captured merchant Sildar Hallwinter.
- Losing conditions: Killed in combat, drowned in the cave flood trap, overwhelmed by goblin ambush.

## ACT 1: CHARACTER CREATION & THE AMBUSH SITE

### SCENE 1 — THE TRIBOAR TRAIL (Opening)
NARRATIVE: The player travels along the Triboar Trail, a dusty road winding through forested hills. 
They come upon a disturbing scene: two dead horses lie in the road, riddled with crude black-feathered arrows. 
A wrecked supply wagon is overturned nearby. Fresh goblin tracks lead north into the dense forest.
INSTRUCTION: Player chooses their class NOW. Options:
- Fighter → (HP: 10+CON mod, AC: 16 chain mail + shield, Proficiencies: all weapons/armor)
- Rogue → (HP: 8+CON mod, AC: 14 leather + DEX, Proficiencies: light armor, finesse/ranged, thieves' tools)
- Wizard → (HP: 6+CON mod, AC: 12 Mage Armor, Spell Slots: 2 first-level, Proficiencies: arcane focus, daggers, light crossbow)
- Cleric → (HP: 8+CON mod, AC: 18 chain mail + shield, Spell Slots: 2 first-level, Proficiencies: medium armor, shields, simple weapons)

### SCENE 2 — ABILITY SCORES
After class selection, player assigns or rolls ability scores.
Standard Array: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8 assigned to STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA.
Calculate modifiers: (score - 10) / 2, rounded down.
Calculate AC and HP from class + CON modifier.
Equip starting gear by class.

## ACT 2: THE GOBLIN TRAIL

### SCENE 3 — FOLLOWING THE TRACKS
The goblin trail leads through thick forest. After about ten minutes of travel:
- Perception check (DC 12): Success → spot a hidden snare trap ahead.
- Failure → trigger the trap (DEX save DC 10, failure = 1d6 damage, restrained until freed).
Continue north. A stream runs alongside the trail.

### SCENE 4 — GOBLIN SENTRIES
Two goblin sentries hide in the thicket near the cave entrance.
- Stealth check (DC 10) to approach unseen.
- If spotted: Goblins attack! Initiative order.
- Goblin stats: AC 15 (leather + shield), HP 7, Scimitar +4 (1d6+2), Shortbow +4 (1d6+2).

## ACT 3: THE CAVE

### CAVE AREA 1 — ENTRANCE
A shallow stream flows out of the cave mouth. Thick briars flank either side.
- Inside: dim light from a fire deeper in. The passage is 5 feet wide, 8 feet tall.
- Perception DC 15: Notice wolf chains and scratching marks near the first bend.

### CAVE AREA 2 — WOLF DEN
Three wolves are chained in an alcove. They snarl and lunge at intruders.
- Animal Handling DC 15: Calm the wolves. They become passive.
- Failure: Wolves attack (AC 13, HP 11, Bite +4 1d6+2, trip DC 11 STR or prone).
- A narrow chimney in the ceiling leads up to Area 5 (Klarg's cave). Climb DC 10 Athletics.

### CAVE AREA 3 — FLOOD PASSAGE
A dam of logs and debris blocks a pool of water in a side cave.
- If players make noise or fight here, a goblin on a bridge above releases the dam.
- DEX save DC 10 or be swept 30 feet toward the cave entrance, taking 1d6 bludgeoning.
- The water level drops, revealing a passage to Area 4.

### CAVE AREA 4 — GOBLIN BARRACKS
Six goblins lounge around a cooking fire. Their leader Yeemik (a tougher goblin, HP 12) holds a human captive — Sildar Hallwinter.
- Yeemik's demand: "Kill Klarg and I'll let the human go."
- Options: Negotiate, fight, or sneak past to Area 5.
- If combat: 6 goblins (as above). Yeemik will use Sildar as a hostage (threatens to push him off a ledge).

### CAVE AREA 5 — KLARG'S CAVE (BOSS)
A large natural cave. A bugbear (Klarg) and his pet wolf guard stolen supplies.
- Klarg stats: AC 16 (hide + shield), HP 27, Morningstar +4 (2d8+2), Javelin +2 (1d6+2).
- Klarg's wolf: Same as wolf den wolves.
- Stealth approach possible: Surprise round if party is undetected.
- A treasure chest holds 600 cp, 110 sp, two potions of healing, and a jade statuette (40 gp).

## KEY NPCs
- Sildar Hallwinter — Human warrior, captured by the goblins. Member of the Lords' Alliance. Will reward the player and share information about Phandalin if rescued.
- Klarg — Bugbear leader of the cave goblins. Cruel and overconfident. Serves a mysterious figure called "the Black Spider."
- Yeemik — Ambitious goblin second-in-command. Wants to overthrow Klarg.
