# Module: Alone Against the Flames
# Author: Gavin Inglis | CoC 7th Edition Solo Adventure
# Setting: 1920s New England, village of Emberhead, New Hampshire

## OVERVIEW & TONE
- The player is STRANDED in the sinister village of Emberhead.
- The villagers plan to sacrifice a traveler at their annual Beacon festival — THE PLAYER.
- Core mystery: The village performs a yearly ritual at an iron Beacon to appease fire entities from space (Cthugha's servants).
- Winning condition: Escape Emberhead alive before or during the Festival night.
- Losing conditions: Burned at the Beacon; killed by villagers, bear, or fall damage; burned to death while chained.

## ACT 2: STRANDED IN EMBERHEAD (Day 1 Exploration)
### KEY FACTS ABOUT EMBERHEAD
- Small hilltop village, surrounded by cliffs, only 2 roads (west = arrival, east = escape route)
- Population: ~18 households, everyone is complicit in the ritual
- The BEACON: large iron structure at the northeast of the village, used to summon fire entities from space and sacrifice a traveler
- Festival: torch-lit procession, lighting the Beacon — happens on the 2nd night after arrival
- The real truth (from NPC Arbogast): it's not sacrifice but a ritual of CONTROL — the villagers don't understand what they're actually summoning

### KEY NPCs
- Silas — Bus driver, brought the player to Emberhead deliberately.
- May Ledbetter — Widow, runs boarding house. Warm but complicit. Will drug player's room if given chance.
- Ruth Ledbetter — May's 10-year-old daughter. Knows what the festival really means. Will warn player if alone with them.
- Clyde Winters — Village hall administrator. The festival's organizer/high priest figure. Has occult texts.
- Arbogast — Scarred, burned old man living on lower ridge. Former "conduit" for the ritual. Will teach player the chant to end the ritual.

### §4 — MAY'S BOARDING HOUSE
